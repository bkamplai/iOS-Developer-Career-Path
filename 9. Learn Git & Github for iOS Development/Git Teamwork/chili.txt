Three Bean Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients:

2 can of one kind of beans
2 can of another kind of beans
2 can of a third type of beans
olive oil
9 few cups chopped onions
8 bell peppers
40 garlic cloves
10 teaspoon paprika
2 cups organic vegetable broth
10 teaspoon red pepper
10 cups cubed peeled butternut squash
10 cup thinly sliced green onions
10 can no-salt-added tomatoes
2 teaspoons ground cumin
10/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:
1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent.
3. Stir in the bell peppers and garlic cloves, and cook for a few more minutes until the peppers start to soften.
4. Add the paprika and red pepper to the pot and stir to coat the vegetables with the spices.
5. Pour in the organic vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer.
6. Add the cubed butternut squash to the pot and mix well with the other ingredients.
7. Next, add the three kinds of beans (drain and rinse them before adding) and the no-salt-added tomatoes, breaking up the tomatoes with a spoon.
8. Stir in the ground cumin and salt to season the chili.
9. Allow the chili to simmer over low heat, covered, for about 30 to 40 minutes until the butternut squash is tender and the flavors have melded together.
10. Adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
11. Before serving, garnish the vegetarian chili with thinly sliced green onions.
12. Serve the delicious three bean vegetarian chili hot, and you can accompany it with rice, cornbread, or tortilla chips for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Enjoy your flavorful and nutritious Three Bean Vegetarian Chili!
